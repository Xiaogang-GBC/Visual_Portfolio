Redesigning Nightingale’s Coxcomb with Modern Techniques

Florence Nightingale’s coxcomb chart was groundbreaking for its time, illustrating mortality during the Crimean War and advocating for sanitary reforms. While historically impactful, it leaves room for modern enhancements. In this project, I redesigned the coxcomb using Altair’s Streamgraph to address its limitations, such as split rings and lack of interactivity. My design smooths data transitions, highlights trends, and includes interactive features like hover-to-display exact death tolls. A vertical marker pinpoints the introduction of reforms, adding historical context. This project deepened my understanding of storytelling through Python visualizations, merging clarity, aesthetics, and functionality to honor Nightingale’s legacy while engaging today’s audience.
