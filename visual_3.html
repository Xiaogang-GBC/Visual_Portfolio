Crypto’s Growing Influence in the 2024 U.S. Election

This infographic examines the increasing impact of cryptocurrency on the 2024 U.S. election. Highlighting rapid growth in crypto ownership and corporate contributions, it reveals a shift toward Republican support in key crypto-holding states. The visualization compares political leanings of top crypto companies with traditional corporations, providing insights into evolving political dynamics. Through clear and engaging visuals, this work sheds light on how digital currencies are reshaping the political landscape.
